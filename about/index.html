About LiTong Group

Leading authorized distributor of Microchip Technology products with global reach and technical expertise.

Our Company

LiTong Group is an officially authorized Level 1 distributor of Microchip Technology, providing genuine products, technical support, and supply chain services to customers worldwide. With over 20 years of experience in the electronics industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, technical excellence, and customer service.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Microchip's innovative semiconductor solutions and the global electronics manufacturing community by providing authentic products, comprehensive technical support, and efficient supply chain services.

Our Technical Team

Our team of field application engineers (FAEs) provides comprehensive technical support throughout the design cycle:

Design Support

  • Component selection guidance
  • Reference design recommendations
  • Technical consultation

Development Support

  • Firmware and software support
  • Development tool guidance
  • Debugging assistance

Production Support

  • Volume pricing and supply assurance
  • Production planning consultation
  • Quality and reliability support

Our FAEs have deep expertise in Microchip product families including PIC microcontrollers, AVR microcontrollers, SAM MCUs, FPGAs, analog devices, and memory products. They provide technical resources including application notes, reference designs, and implementation guides.
