Automotive Touch HMI Solution

Complete Human Machine Interface solution for automotive applications using Microchip's mTouch technology for reliable and responsive touch control.

Solution Overview

This automotive touch HMI solution leverages Microchip's mTouch technology to deliver a responsive, reliable, and safe interface for automotive applications. The solution includes capacitive touch sensing, display control, and safety features that meet automotive industry standards.

Technical Challenge

Automotive interfaces must operate reliably in challenging environments with temperature extremes, electromagnetic interference, and safety requirements. Touch interfaces must provide accurate detection while maintaining safety standards for driver distraction and system reliability.

Microchip Solution

Our solution combines Microchip's PIC MCUs with mTouch technology to provide robust capacitive touch sensing capabilities. The solution features:

  • Capacitive touch sensing with mTouch technology
  • Hardware-based touch sensing for consistent performance
  • Configurable touch zones and gestures
  • Temperature and humidity compensation
  • EMI immunity for automotive environments
  • ASIL compliance capabilities
