Technical Challenge

Industrial control systems must operate reliably in harsh environments with temperature extremes, electrical noise, vibration, and long operational life requirements. These systems must provide deterministic response times, high reliability, and extensive diagnostic capabilities while supporting various industrial communication protocols.

Microchip Solution

Our solution combines Microchip's microcontrollers, FPGAs, and analog components to provide robust industrial control capabilities. The solution features:

  • High-reliability microcontrollers with extended temperature ranges
  • Integrated communication interfaces (CAN, Ethernet, industrial protocols)
  • Advanced analog signal conditioning for sensor inputs
  • Deterministic processing for real-time control
  • Extensive diagnostic and monitoring capabilities
  • Security features for industrial cybersecurity
