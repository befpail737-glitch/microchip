Technical Challenge

IoT devices must operate reliably in diverse environments while maintaining security, managing power consumption, and connecting to the internet through various wireless protocols. These devices must collect, process, and transmit data efficiently while being cost-effective and scalable.

Microchip Solution

Our solution combines Microchip's microcontrollers, connectivity devices, and security components to provide comprehensive IoT capabilities. The solution features:

  • Ultra-low power microcontrollers for extended battery life
  • Integrated connectivity options (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, Cellular)
  • Hardware security features for data protection
  • Scalable architectures from sensors to gateways
  • Comprehensive development tools and middleware
  • Cloud connectivity and management capabilities
