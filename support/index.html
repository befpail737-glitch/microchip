Common Technical Questions

How do I request a sample of a Microchip part?

You can request samples directly through our website or by contacting your local sales representative. For new customers, we may require verification of your company and intended use. Sample requests are typically processed within 1-2 business days.

Where can I find the latest datasheets?

Datasheets are available on Microchip's official website (microchip.com) and through our technical resources section. If you need help locating a specific document, contact our support team and we'll provide a direct link.

What's the difference between PIC and AVR microcontrollers?

PIC microcontrollers are known for their wide product portfolio and strong integration, while AVR microcontrollers are recognized for their ease of use and excellent open-source toolchain support. Both offer excellent technical support and resources through our FAE team.

How can I ensure long-term supply of my selected parts?

We provide supply chain assurance for all Microchip products. For critical designs, we recommend parts that have extended availability commitments from Microchip. Contact our support team for a lifecycle analysis of your selected components.
