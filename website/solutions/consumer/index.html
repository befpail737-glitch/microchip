Technical Challenge

Consumer electronics must balance performance, features, and cost while meeting demanding power requirements for battery operation. These devices must be manufactured cost-effectively, meet safety standards, and provide intuitive user interfaces while maintaining reliability and quality across high-volume production.

Microchip Solution

Our solution combines Microchip's microcontrollers, analog components, and connectivity devices to provide comprehensive consumer electronics capabilities. The solution features:

  • Cost-effective microcontrollers for optimal BOM optimization
  • Integrated peripherals for user interfaces (touch, display, audio)
  • Low power operation for battery-powered applications
  • Integrated connectivity options (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi)
  • Comprehensive development tools and reference designs
  • Long-term product availability and support
