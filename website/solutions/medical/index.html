Technical Challenge

Medical devices must meet strict safety, reliability, and regulatory requirements including FDA, IEC 60601, and ISO 14155 standards. These devices must operate reliably for patient safety, provide high-precision measurements, maintain data integrity, and meet electromagnetic compatibility requirements in clinical environments.

Microchip Solution

Our solution combines Microchip's safety-certified microcontrollers, precision analog components, and secure connectivity to provide comprehensive medical device capabilities. The solution features:

  • Safety-certified microcontrollers with self-test capabilities
  • High-precision analog front-ends for accurate measurements
  • Security features for patient data protection
  • Low power operation for portable and implantable devices
  • Comprehensive documentation for regulatory compliance
  • Long-term product availability and support
